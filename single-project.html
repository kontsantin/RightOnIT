Vector Moving Company Web Design

Transforming a local moving company's online presence

A complete redesign focused on usability, conversions, and modern aesthetics

Client

Vector Moving Company - San Diego

Services

Web Design, UX/UI, Frontend Development

Timeline

6 weeks

Technologies

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, WordPress

Project Details

The Challenge

Vector Moving Company approached us with an outdated HTML website that was poorly converting visitors into customers. The existing site had several pain points:

  • Dated design that didn't reflect their professional services
  • No mobile responsiveness
  • Complex navigation structure
  • No online booking or quote system
  • Slow loading times

Our Solution

We completely reimagined their digital presence with a modern, conversion-focused design:

Custom Video Header

Created an engaging full-screen video background showing their team in action to immediately connect with visitors.

Interactive Quote Calculator

Developed a dynamic form that provides instant moving estimates based on home size and services needed.

Service Showcase

Redesigned their service pages with clear icons, before/after sliders, and customer testimonials.

Mobile-First Approach

Ensured perfect display and functionality across all devices with responsive breakpoints.
